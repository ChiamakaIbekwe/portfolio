About Me

My name is Chiamaka Boma Ibekwe and I was born in the year 2002 in a government hospital in Port Harcourt, Rivers State Nigeria.

I started Kindergarten at age 2 (year 2004) at Kina Education Center at Woji Port Harcourt.

I started middle school at Shiloh Hills Primary School at Rumuibekwe Port Harcourt, briefly and moved to Shalom Child Education were I finished Middle School.

I preceded to Junior High School at Oasis Global Academy at Rumigbo axis of Port Harcourt. Then left for Redeemer's International High School at Abuloma, Rivers State to commence Senior High.

Later, I attended Shiloh Hills High School were I studied and took the West African Senior Secondary Examination(WASSCE) and came out with 5 credits, including English and Mathematics respectively

Currently, I am a free Lancer and an online outsourcer.
