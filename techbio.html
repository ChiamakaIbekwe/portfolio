Tech Bio

I really didn't know what to study while growing up, firstly I thought of being a Teacher then an Engineer and then a Mathematician! All of these were good but I still felt as though something was missing... that was when I got into a program known as Virtual Labour Export

This program changed my life! I had a sense of direction and "pride"- if you may say.

This program gave me the experience I needed to be intermediate in Web Design and Development and also Graphic Design.

As I mentioned, currently I am a freelancer and Online outsourcer. I also follow my passion which is tutoring and harnessing young talents- just the way I was! I also plan to go to College too. Looking forward to Massachusette Institute of Technology (MIT).
